Engineering a Greener World!

GSI’s control system uses state-of-the-art technology to provide a complete solution for monitoring and controlling the entire power system.


Combining LED lights with our SmartControl system reduces costs, enhances energy efficiency, and provides 24/7 monitoring when utilizing cloud computing infrastructure.


The GSI Advantage

  • Accurate, real time monitoring system and historical data
  • Reduce overall installation and maintenance costs
  • Increase energy efficiency and reduce power consumption

Why Us?

1.

Our Mission

Our mission is to implement energy efficient solutions, as well as Solid State Lighting LED Technology. We would also like to integrate control systems and sensors where applicable to maximize energy saving. The use and implementation of cloud technologies and various forms of data collection platforms are also apart of our goal, in order to support Stipulated Savings Models.

3.

Our Process

Our process includes identifying clients' needs and goals, performing audits and evaluating our clients' current control and lighting systems, assessing the feasibility of upgrading or modifying our clients' control and lighting system, as well as the feasibility of control or sensor integration with current or new lighting systems. In addition to these preliminary steps, we work to provide financial, engineering and other required services in order to ensure the conversion of technologies is most efficient and beneficial to it's recipeints.

2.

Our Strategy

In order to have a successful business and efficient implementation of green technologies, our strategy is to have a team of qualified professionals with years of experience in the field, as well as to maintain exclusive relationships with Validated Manufufacturers of SSL/LED.
